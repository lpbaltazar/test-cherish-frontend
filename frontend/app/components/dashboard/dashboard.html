Instructions

Unaided Evaluation
  1. Click Unaided Track in the side navigator.
  2. Select CXR image to evaluate.
  3. Evaluate CXR image and Patient Data (age, and sex) by entering notes and diagnosis.
  4. Repeat for all CXR image.
Aided Evaluation
  1. Click Aided Track in the side navigator.
  2. Select CXR image to evaluate.
  3. Evaluate CXR image, Patient Data (age, and sex), and Risk Scores by entering notes and diagnosis.
  4. Repeat for all CXR image.

Notes

  • Radiologist can only proceed to the Aided track after evaluation of all CXR images (216 instances).
  • Editing of the diagnosis is not allowed once the radiologist change patients.
  • Diagnostic classes are: Normal, Abnormal non-pneumonia, Non-COVID Pneumonia, COVID-Pneumonia
  • Doctor-to-doctor consultation is NOT allowed
  • Images are shuffled in preparation for AI Aided session
